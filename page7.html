Trip Enhacements

Besides the experiences at the farmstead in the heart of the Andes, Vivacs del Plata has curated a selection of extraordinary activities in other breathtaking locations throughout Mendoza. Whether you're staying with us or elsewhere, you can still participate in these captivating adventures.

Sailing

Location: Potrerillos Dam (30 minutes away
from Vivacs del Plata)

Availability: All year (subject to climate
conditions)

Hop on board our sailboat “Prana” and
experience the beauty of Potrerillos Dam, a
stunning water mirror surrounded by desertic
mountains. Helmed by Patrón Pedro, you’ll
sail on a 24.5 ft MASTRACCHIO boat, while
relishing the picturesque views and indulging
in wine and snacks.

Sailing trip: USD 130 per crew member

Round-trip transfer: USD 100 4 PAX max

Motorcycle road trip

Location: Mendoza province and surroundings

Availability: Throughout the year

At Vivacs del Plata, we have a deep passion
for outdoor activities, and motorcycling
holds a special place in our hearts. Mendoza
offers the perfect combination of weather,
roads, and landscapes for an unforgettable
motorcycle road trip. Choose between KTM or
BMW GS motorcycles and let our experienced
guides show you the hidden gems of
Mendoza’s mountains.

Golf outing

ATAMISQUE
+

Location: Atamisque, San José, Tupungato (20 minutes away from Vivacs del Plata)

Play 9 Holes of Par 3 Bliss, just 20 Minutes from Vivacs del Plata in a guided visit. Transportation is included if you are staying at Vivacs del Plata. Immerse yourself in the scenic beauty of the Atamisque golf course.

Green fee: USD 100 per player

Caddie: USD 75 per bag (double bag)

CLUB DE CAMPO
+

Location: Guaymallen, Mendoza (1 hr 20 min away from Vivacs del Plata )

Play in the only 18 holes yard of Mendoza guided by skilled caddy scratch players who are esteemed club members. The course is par 72, has 6700 yards, and a stunning view to The Andes Mountain range.

Green fee: U$D 100 per player

Caddie: USD 75 per bag (double bag) or USD 100 per bag (simple bag)

Round-trip transfer: U$D 120 4 PAX max
