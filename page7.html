Trip Enhacements

Besides the experiences at the farmstead in the heart of the Andes, Vivacs del Plata has curated a selection of extraordinary activities in other breathtaking locations throughout Mendoza. Whether you're staying with us or elsewhere, you can still participate in these captivating adventures.

Sailing

Location: Potrerillos Dam (30 minutes away from Vivacs del Plata)

Availability: All year (subject to climate conditions)

Hop on board our sailboat “Prana” and experience the beauty of Potrerillos Dam, a stunning water mirror surrounded by desertic mountains. Helmed by Captain Pedro, you’ll sail on a 24.5 ft MASTRACCHIO boat, while relishing the picturesque views and indulging in wine and snacks.

Sailing trip: USD 130 per crew member

Round-trip transfer: USD 100 4 PAX max

Motorcycle road trip

Location: Mendoza province and surroundings

Availability: Throughout the year

At Vivacs del Plata, we have a deep passion for outdoor activities, and motorcycling holds a special place in our hearts. Mendoza offers the perfect combination of weather, roads, and landscapes for an unforgettable motorcycle road trip. Choose between KTM or BMW GS motorcycles and let our experienced guides show you the hidden gems of Mendoza’s mountains.-

Play 9 Holes of Par 3 Bliss, just 20 Minutes from Vivacs del Plata in a guided visit. Transportation is included if you are staying at Vivacs del Plata. Immerse yourself in the scenic beauty of the Atamisque golf course.
