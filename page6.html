All Inclusive Experience

We strive to make your experience effortless and unforgettable. From horseback riding through rolling hills to hiking along picturesque trails, you can discover enchanting landscapes at your own pace. Engage in exciting farm tours, learn about our traditional practices, or simply relax amidst the serenity of nature.

Activities

  • HORSEBACK RIDING

    Experiencie the thrill of our guest-favorite horseback riding tour! Let us guide you through 400 hectares of Vivacs del Plata, where you'll encounter diverse flora and fauna, surrounded by the natural beauty of the mountains. Choose between a short ride of 45 minutes or opt for the long option, which takes you on a captivating journey lasting 3 hours.

  • 4X4 GUIDED TOUR

    Sit back and relax as our experienced drivers take you on a guided 4x4 tour of Vivacs del Plata. Explore the vast landscape, from the ranch itself to the majestic mountains, all from the comfort of your seat.

  • CYCLING

    For an unparalleled cycling experience in nature at an altitude of 2,100 meters above sea level, Vivacs del Plata is a dream come true. While it can be challenging at times, the rewards of cycling in the Andes are truly worth the effort. Our knowledgeable guide will accompany you and select the trail that best matches your ability.

  • TREKKING

    Unleash your adventurous spirit and immerse yourself in nature by embarking on a trekking adventure through the prairies and hills of Vivacs del Plata. It's an excellent opportunity to find tranquility and engage in meditation. You can request a guide or, go exploring by yourself.

Culinary Experience

  • Whether you crave traditional Argentinean barbecue, fresh vegetables, or exquisite local wines, every meal is a celebration of our rich gastronomic heritage.

  • The culinary journey is led by our talented chef who is not only an experienced “asador” but also a true mountain man.

  • With a deep understanding of the art of grilling our chef brings a unique blend of expertise and passion to each culinary creation.

  • While beef is the quintessential choice at Vivacs del Plata, we are delighted to accommodate your preferences with options such as lamb, pork, fish or goat, provided you inform us in advance.

  • We understand the importance of catering to individual dietary needs, and we are pleased to offer vegetarian options for guests who prefer plant-based delights. Simply let us know about any special dietary requirements, and we will ensure that your culinary experience is tailored to your preferences.

  • Rest assured, all of these delectable dining experiences are included under the daily rate, allowing you to indulge in a range of culinary delights without any additional cost.
